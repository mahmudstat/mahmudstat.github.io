# Reading

- 3 or 4 reading passages of about 700 words each. 
- For each passage, there are 10 multiple-choice questions.
- Most questions have four choices and a single correct answer. Some questions ask you to select two or more correct answers from a larger group of choices.
- 54 to 72 minutes to complete the Reading 
- Can return to previous questions to review or change your answers.

## How to approach

- See the big picture
- Read title
- Read illustrations
- Identify topic
- Skim (Glance first few sentences)
- Read one line from each para (main points of each para, author's intent)
- Read last para
- Take notes
- Read whole passage
- Understand why
- Then look at the questions and go back to passage if needed (find keywords, scanning)
- Look for keywords from question

## Tips

1.  Synthesize information presented in the text 
2.  Identify the author’s rhetorical purpose
3.  Scan the text to find specific pieces of information

Prose Summary questions ask you to select the major ideas from a passage by distinguishing them from minor points or ideas that are not in the passage. Fill in a Table questions ask you to correctly categorize facts presented in the passage.

# Speaking

17 minutes

## Question 1

Independent speaking task: designed to measure your ability to speak English effectively in academic settings

- Two situtations
- Explain your choice
- Use prep to time organize choice (WRITE KEYWORDS/Reasons)
- Don't write full response (no time)
- Speak naturally and use connecting words
- Use: because, so, after that, on the other hand, I want to mention, what this means is that
- Total 4 marks (0-4)

### Criteria

#### Delivery

- clear and fluid 
- good pronunciation
- natural pace
- good intonation

#### Language Use

- use of grammar and vocabulary

#### Topic Development

- How fully you answer
- How clearly you express
- How you can connect

### Tips

- Practice speaking continusouly for 45 seconds or more
- Speak with natives/good speakers
- Collect pictures and describe in 45 seconds
- Try describing same picture with different words
- Think for 15 seconds aout what you did yesterday (45 seconds)
- Do same for what you will do tomorrow (45 seconds)
- Practice making recommendations and explain why this idea is good
